About Me

I am Zedekiah Ambogo, a passionate data scientist based in Machakos, Kenya. I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from Machakos University, where I graduated with Second Class Honors (Upper Division). During my academic journey, I gained extensive knowledge in data analysis, software engineering, operating systems, data structures and algorithms, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and statistics.

In addition to my academic achievements, I have hands-on experience from my internships at HIVE and the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics. My work includes developing a Sanskrit Text-to-Speech system, aggregating unstructured data for new product development, and analyzing data to guide marketing strategies.
